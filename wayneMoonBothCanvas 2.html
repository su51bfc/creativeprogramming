My theme is a skyline, including a church on a clear night with the moon passing by shimmering in the background. I created this by using HTML5 canvas. By creating a blue canvas and then creating an overlay of a bottom green rectangle I had drawn a basic page background for my buildings to sit on. I created various black rectangles to create the skyline. On top of the second rectangle I drew a triangle using an example from the example code. On top of the black triangles I created small bright yellow rectangles to emulate lights being left on inside the buildings. On the building that looks like a church I made a doorway out of a grey rectangle and then placed two black rectangles in the shape of a cross. To get all the shapes in the correct place I experimented with the coordinates to make sure they were all lined up correctly. The moon was created using the ‘arc’ and math.pi *2 with a fill style of silver so it appears to shine brightly in the sky. I used the lineTo, moveTo and closePath method to draw the triangle. I used fillStyle and fillRect to colour the rectangles. To animate the moon I created a variable and a function to move the moon across the skyline. I used the function to set the start position of the moon on the canvas and the setInterval function to control the speed of movement. I wanted the moon to rise diagonally across the canvas, however, I had difficulty setting the correct coordinates to create the desired effect. I am still pleased with the overall look of the animated canvas.
